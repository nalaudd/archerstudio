Archer Studio

What is this place?

This web application is designed for my personal use. It allows me to able to keep track of my work schedule and my custom schedule. One may ask, "Why would I need to keep track of my work schedule if I work a 9-5 job?" The reason is because there was a time when I was not in a programming job. When I was in retail I would receive a schedule that would rotate around shifts.

This is the reason why I made this application in this manner. The custom schedule is for other things such as appointments, school or extra activities. Before working at Revature I felt my programming knowledge was not at the level that I wanted. With the training programming I was exposed to technologies that even some companies have not even adopted yet.

Archer Studio is a web application that is a composit of all of my past projects assembled into one large project. You will even have the ability to sign up and login into your account for your needs.
